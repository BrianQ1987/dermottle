Welcome to America's most difficult game show that asks one simple question: Dylan McDermott or Dermot Mulroney?

Each day, we name a movie and your challenge is to correctly guess whether that movie starred Dylan McDermott or Dermot Mulroney. You only get one guess, so make it count.

  1. Look at today's question card. The name of today's movie will be highlighted in large blue text.
  2. If you think you know whether this movie starred Dylan McDermott or Dermot Mulroney you can make a guess by selecting one of the buttons below the question card. This will end the game for the day, whether you are corrent or not. So choose wisely.
  3. If you need more help you can press any of the "Reveal" buttons on the question card to reveal more information. However, the more hints you reveal the further your score will be from the perfect score (0 hints used).
  4. Don't forget to share your progress andcome back tomorrow and play a whole new round of DERMOTTLE
